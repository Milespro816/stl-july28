Logistics & Notes
Book the Westin Now
Cardinals–Cubs series draws out-of-towners from both cities. July 28 is a Monday game — rooms go fast. Book on Marriott.com directly to earn points and maintain Titanium benefits.
Call Broadway Oyster Bar Today
They don't take OpenTable. Call 314-621-8811 directly and request a 4:30 PM reservation for 3 on Monday July 28. Walk-ins on game nights are long waits.
Daughter's Arrival Logistics
Chicago → STL: ~5 hr drive (I-55 S) or 1 hr Southwest/AA flight into STL Lambert. Add her to the room so she can check in independently if you're out. Confirm room type — two queens preferred if she's sharing.
Titanium Early Check-in
Call the Westin front desk the morning of July 28 (from Sawmill) and request early check-in as a Titanium Elite member. High success rate — especially on a Monday when weekend guests have cleared out.
VIP Suite Access
Confirm suite entry details with your ticket issuer — gate, entry time, and whether credentials are mobile or physical. Suites typically allow entry 90 min before first pitch.
